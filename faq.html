Everything you need to know about challenges, rules, payouts, and trading at iMAX Funded.

◆ Challenges & evaluation
What challenges does iMAX Funded offer?
+
iMAX offers three programs: Discipline Path (2-phase evaluation, from $49), Accelerator (1-phase fast-track, from $79), and Capital Builder (instant funding, from $149). Each is designed for a different level of trader experience and appetite.
What account sizes are available?
+
Account sizes range from $5,000 to $200,000 across Discipline Path and Accelerator programs. Capital Builder offers $10,000 to $100,000. You can scale beyond $200K through our performance-based scaling plan once funded.
Can I retake a challenge if I fail?
+
Yes. You can purchase a new challenge at any time. We also run periodic free retry offers for traders who came close to passing. Check the community Discord for current promotions.
Is there a time limit to complete a challenge?
+
No hard time limit. You can take as long as you need to hit the profit target — as long as you remain within your drawdown limits. This allows you to trade at a pace that suits your strategy and schedule.
▲ Trading rules
Is news trading allowed?
+
Yes. News trading is fully allowed across all challenge and funded account types at iMAX. We believe skilled traders should be able to take advantage of high-impact events.
Can I use Expert Advisors (EAs)?
+
EAs are allowed on the EA Challenge account type specifically built for algorithmic traders. On Discipline Path and Accelerator accounts, EAs are restricted to assist-only functions — they may not be used for full automated execution. Capital Builder does not permit EAs.
Is copy trading allowed?
+
You may copy trades between your own iMAX accounts. Copying trades from external traders or signals is not permitted, as this undermines the evaluation process and discipline-building intent of our programs.
What instruments can I trade?
+
iMAX supports trading across:
  • Forex majors, minors, and exotics
  • Gold (XAU/USD) and Silver
  • Major indices (US30, NAS100, SPX500)
  • Commodities (Oil, Natural Gas)
  • Selected cryptocurrencies
Can I hold trades over the weekend?
+
Yes, overnight and weekend holding is permitted on all account types. We recommend exercising caution around major geopolitical events and market gaps, but we do not restrict when you hold positions.
■ Payouts & profit split
How quickly are payouts processed?
+
Payouts are processed within 24 hours of request for verified accounts. We pay via bank transfer, crypto (USDT/BTC), and Wise. Same-day payouts are available for accounts with verified KYC.
What is the profit split?
+
  • Discipline Path: up to 90% profit split
  • Accelerator: up to 95% profit split
  • Capital Builder: up to 80% profit split
Split increases with scale milestones — consistent performers unlock the highest tier automatically.
When can I request my first payout?
+
Once you are funded, you can request your first payout after 14 calendar days of trading. Subsequent payouts can be requested every 7 days with no minimum profit requirement beyond your split entitlement.
◆ Accounts & platforms
Which trading platforms does iMAX support?
+
iMAX accounts are available on MetaTrader 5 (MT5), TradeLocker, and MatchTrader. All platforms are available on desktop (Windows/Mac) and mobile (iOS/Android).
Can I have multiple funded accounts?
+
Yes. There is no limit on the number of funded accounts you can hold simultaneously. Many iMAX traders manage multiple accounts across different account types and sizes. Aggregate funded capital cap is $2,000,000 per trader.
What leverage is available?
+
  • Challenge phase: up to 1:100
  • Funded phase: up to 1:50 (forex), 1:20 (indices), 1:10 (commodities)
  • EA Challenge: up to 1:100 during challenge
▲ General
Where is iMAX Funded based?
+
iMAX Funded is registered and operated internationally, with operational support teams available globally across time zones. Our support is available 24/7 via live chat, Discord, and email.
Is iMAX Funded regulated?
+
Like all proprietary trading firms, iMAX Funded is not a regulated financial institution. Traders are not investing client funds — they are trading the firm's simulated and live capital under a performance agreement. Always trade responsibly.
How do I contact support?
+
You can reach the iMAX support team via: Live chat on this website (bottom right), Discord community server, or Email at support@imaxfunded.com. Response times are typically under 30 minutes during trading hours.
